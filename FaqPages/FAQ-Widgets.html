Widgets

In the element settings, turn on the Required field option. The required field will be automatically marked with an asterisk
Mouse over the widget that you’d like to copy and click Duplicate in this widget’s settings.
You can set up image attributes (alt and title texts) in the widget's settings
Go to the widget's settings to configure a value of a division
Go to the widget's settings and disable the Numbers in a range option

Method #1

Go to the Design tab and click on the gear icon to proceed to settings. In the window that will appear, you will be able to change the range

Method #2

Go to the Formula tab then edit the range values.
